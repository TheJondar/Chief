  • Our Mission
Founded by Purple Heart Recipient, David Pereira, The Chief Fund is a movement to raise awareness throughout communities of the sacrifices our veteran men and women have made & continue to make. That wounds can be both physical and mental, and that raising funds to help our veterans can help change lives.
Strength comes from being part of a community, being part of a team, and going after a common goal. We use fitness to honor those who have sacrificed themselves because we know serving isn’t for everyone, but service is something we should all be a part of. Be of service to those who have served by challenging yourself physically & mentally while raising funds to support combat wounded and disabled veterans.

Our Services

Physical Fitness and Wellness

We believe you must be fit to fight… Part of that is to ensure that veterans get the proper physical fitness services and wellness solutions they need to make sure they stay in the fight long after they take their uniform off.

Mental Health and Therapy Support

As military service members, we were trained to Operate in high stress environments, Complete tasks, and execute the mission… But we weren't necessarily trained on how to deal with all that once we returned home Or get out of the service.

Personal and Professional Development

The transition from service to civilian life can be extremely stressful and lonely. We want to ensure you build on your attention to detail and discipline as you evolve into civilian life.

Family Support

Because no one serves alone and it takes a team - we want to ensure that your team Has the Necessary services and information to be successful.
